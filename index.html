The Story
WHAT "4 THE TRENCHES" MEANS

This isn't just a tour. It's a homecoming. It's a statement. It's for every block, every corner, every city that raised us. "4 The Trenches" is for the ones who never left and the ones who made it out.

"We doing this for Detroit. For the east side. For everybody who believed in us when nobody else did."

42 Dugg (Dion Hayes) — CMG / 4PF / Interscope. From the Denby neighborhood on Detroit's east side. Released from prison in 2023, dropped 4eva Us Neva Them and Part 3, and never looked back.

Babyface Ray (Marcellus Register) — Wavy Gang / EMPIRE. Team Eastside original. Gold-certified "Ron Artest" with Dugg. Codeine Cowboy cemented him as Detroit's most consistent voice.

Two artists. One city. 29 shows. Detroit to the world and back — the Fillmore, July 31st.

Refund policies vary by venue and ticket provider. Check your confirmation email or the venue's website for their specific refund/exchange policy.
How long does merch take to ship?+
Standard orders ship within 7-14 business days. Tour-exclusive items ship after the tour date. Expedited shipping available at checkout.

Most shows are 18+. Some venues are all-ages. Check the specific venue for age requirements. Valid ID required for entry.

VIP meet & greet packages are available for select dates. Check the ticket purchase page for VIP upgrade options.
